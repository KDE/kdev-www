## DB Setup

create the file sites/default/kdev_db_setup.php with the following contents (adapt them as required):

~~~~~~~~
<?php
$db_url = 'mysql://test@localhost/kdev';
$db_prefix = 'kdev_';
~~~~~~~~

Get a MySQL Dump of the KDevelop website by one of the maintainers, just ask on our mailing list.

## Other Setup

create a folder sites/default/files/ and make it readable for your webserver
