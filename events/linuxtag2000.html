People & KDevelop - Impressions from LinuxTag 2000 at Stuttgart/Germany

Here are some pictures from the KDevelop meeting at the LinuxTag Stuttgart (29.6.-2.7.) At the left picture you can see in the last row from left to right, Walter Tasin from the KDevelop core development, Stephan Uhlmann, he IS Kmud and only one homepage translation behind KDevelop :-), one row before thats Mario Keller, he traveled with us to Stuttgart, not really involved with the KDevelop project, but still a K-user. And last but not least Sandy Meier, our maintainer.
