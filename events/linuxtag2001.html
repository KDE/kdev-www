LinuxTag is a very interesting event in Europe. There its possible that you want sit down for a while to read emails and you are sitting beside wellknown people like Jon Maddog Hall, Eric S. Raymond.
Beside the fair itself, where you can find cool projects like this from the Linux Group Japan (Linux on Dreamcast), the LinuxTag offers a good conference programm to the visitors.
More informations about LinuxTag you can find here.
