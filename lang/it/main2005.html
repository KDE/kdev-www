29. November 2005 - KDevelop 3.3 got released to the public together with KDE 3.5, read all about it on the public release announcement. You can download it and see it's Changelog. It has been translated to 22 languages, the user manual has been translated to 8 languages.

11. November 2005 - KDevelop 3.3 release candidate 1 (aka 3.3-rc1) got released to the public together with KDE 3.5 rc1. You can download it and see its Changelog.

18. October 2005 - KDevelop 3.3 beta 2 (aka 3.2.92) got released to the public together with KDE 3.5 beta 2. You can download it and see its Changelog.

13. October 2005 - KDevelop 3.2.3 got released to the public together with KDE 3.4.3. You can download it and see its Changelog.

21. September 2005 - KDevelop 3.3 beta 1 (aka 3.2.91) got released to the public together with KDE 3.5 beta 1. You can download it and see its Changelog.

11. August 2005 - KDevelop 3.3 alpha 1 (aka 3.2.90) got released to the public together with KDE 3.5 alpha 1. You can download it and see its Changelog. KDevelop 3.3 runs on KDE >= 3.2 and QT >= 3.2 but can be used to develop both QT3 and QT4 appplications.
KDevelop HEAD (SVN trunk) now requires QT4 and is planed for release as KDevelop 4.0 together with KDE 4.0 by the end of next year.

28. July 2005 - KDevelop 3.2.2 got released to the public together with KDE 3.4.2. You can download it and see its Changelog.

31. May 2005 - KDevelop 3.2.1 got released to the public together with KDE 3.4.1, read all about it on the public release announcement. You can download it and see its Changelog. It has been translated to 19 languages, the user manual has been translated to 7 languages and this website is available in 12 languages.

21. May 2005 - We have two new Conference sponsors! Trolltech helps KDevelop developers reduce travel expenses and WNet is providing a high-speed internet for the time of the conference. The KDevelop Team thanks our sponsors!

Buon compleanno kdevelop.org.

09. Febbraio 2005 - KDevelop 3.2 beta 2 noto anche come 3.1.92 è stato rilasciato. Puoi scaricarlo e vedere il suo Changelog. e avere un'anteprima delle nuove caratteristiche.

06. Febbraio 2005 - KDevelop ha vinto il premio LinuxQuestions.org Members' Choice Award come IDE dell'anno per il 2004.

13. Gennaio 2005 - KDevelop 3.2 beta 1, conosciuto anche come 3.1.91, è stato rilasciato. Puoi scaricarla e vedere il suo Changelog. I Prossimi rilasci di KDevelop 3.2.x seguiranno il piano di rilascio di KDE 3.4.
Questa è anche il primo ciclo di rilasci della nuova Piattaforma KDevelop API e il primo rilascio dove il files HEAD/ del sito web sono brancheable. Questo significa meno lavoro sia per i traduttori che per il webmaster. I traduttori devono solo tradurre i files in HEAD/ a il sito crea i files del ramo stabile usando qualche magia PHP.

03. Gennaio 2005 - Vota per KDevelop su LinuxQuestions.org nella categoria IDE dell'Anno.
