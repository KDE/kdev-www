implemented for the cpp language part at the moment. They can be configured to
open all header/source files in split view mode and the actual split view can
be configured to orient horizontally, or vertically. This required some pretty

your headers and source files will scroll and remain in sync as you navigate
around your various methods. CCMAIL: kdevelop-devel at kdevelop.org
