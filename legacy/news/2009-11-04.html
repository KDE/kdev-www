KDevelop 4.0 beta6 released

KDevelop 4.0 beta6 (3.9.96) and KDevPlatform 0.9.96 were released today and can be downloaded via public ftp. They fix more than 74 bugs and introduce a well working debugger. Before updating make sure to see the feature comparison with KDevelop3. This version requires at least KDE 4.3 and Qt 4.5. Future releases will follow the KDE 4.4 release schedule
