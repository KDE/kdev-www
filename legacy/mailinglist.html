Since Feb 1999 we have a mailinglist (kdevelop at kdevelop.org) and most of the time 600-700 subscribers. This list is for general discussion and translations.
Mailinglist Archive at: barney.cs.uni-potsdam.de
Thanks to Hank Leininger.

We also have two other lists, the kdevelop-devel list for developers of KDevelop itself, and kdevelop-bugs for the bug report traffic from bugs.kde.org. You can subscribe and unsubscribe to these lists using the form on the top of this page.

The messages to these lists can be found at kdevelop-devel mailinglist archive and kdevelop-bugs mailing list archive.

There is also a possibility to subscribe to the list of KDevelop SVN commits.
