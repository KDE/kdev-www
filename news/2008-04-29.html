First alpha release of KDevelop 4.0
KDevelop 4.0 has been under development since 2005 and is a complete rewrite from scratch. It has a lot of new features that were hard or impossible to achieve with the KDevelop 3.x codebase, but it also has some regressions. Today KDevelop 4.0 alpha 1 (3.90.0) got released together with KDE 4.1 alpha 1. Development is moving very fast, as you can see in the post alpha 1 commit logs.
